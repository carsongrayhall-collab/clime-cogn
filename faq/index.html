How do I request a cancellation?

If your purchase was made less than one business day before your cancellation request, simply click the cancel order button on your confirmation email. For more information on our return policy, please see point 9 - Cancellations, Returns, and Refunds on our Terms and Conditions page, which customers agree to before purchasing any product

What allergens may be contained in Clime’s products?

Clime takes health and safety as it’s highest priority. As such, please do not purchase any of our products if you have a known allergy to any of the ingredients listed on the containers. If you think you may have an allergy to any of our ingredients, please consult a doctor or healthcare professional before purchasing or consuming any Clime products. Our processing facilities may include past contact with our other products or lines of known treenuts, peanuts, or other plant-derived allergens. Therefore, if you have a known allergy to any of these, please consult a doctor before consuming any of Clime’s products. For more information on our return policy, please see point 10 - Allergens and User Responsibility on our Terms and Conditions page, which customers agree to before purchasing any product

I am currently taking a prescription pharmaceutical or frequently over the counter medication. Is it safe to consumer Clime’s products?

Our team strongly advises consulting a doctor or healthcare professional before consuming any of our products if you are currently taking prescription or over the counter drugs. Do not consume any of our products while taking prescribed or over the counter pharmaceuticals. For more information on our return policy, please see point 2 - Products and Intended Use on our Terms and Conditions page, which customers agree to before purchasing any product.
