Our Story

Prince and Carson met during a French immersion course in the heart of Paris. At the time, Prince was evolving his fitness agency tailored towards international clientele. At the same time, Carson was navigating cross-border medical care for American expats and government diplomatic ministries. During this course, both Prince and Carson shared their shared passions for creating a healthier community abroad and at home. For several years, Prince had leveraged his fitness company to formulate a supplement regimen to support his clients. For years both in the United States and France, Prince had been improving several formulas for brain fog, focus, and stamina. Upon discussion, they found a shared enthusiasm for holistic health. With Prince as the visionary and Carson in charge of operations, they launched Clime in late 2025.
