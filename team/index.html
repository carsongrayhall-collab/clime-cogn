Our Story

Clime was forged in Paris, where founders Prince and Carson crossed paths. Prince was directing an international fitness agency—developing custom, targeted formulations to enhance his clients' cognitive and physical stamina—while Carson was managing cross-border healthcare operations for American expats and diplomatic personnel. Driven by a shared mission to scale human optimization, they combined forces. Uniting Prince's rigorous proprietary formulations with Carson's operational infrastructure, they launched Clime in late 2025 to deliver premium, science-led cognitive endurance to the global stage.
